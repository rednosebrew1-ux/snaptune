⚠️ This page uses your camera

SnapTune turns on your device camera and microphone to record a short reaction clip of you while a track plays. You'll always see your own live video feed on screen so you know exactly when it's active.

  • Nothing is uploaded automatically — every clip is generated in your browser only.
  • After each clip, you can watch it back, download it, delete it, or send it to the site owner using your device's own share menu — no cloud storage involved.
  • You can revoke camera access at any time in your browser settings.
